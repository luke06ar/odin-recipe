Description

Empanadas are one of the most popular cultural foods in Argentina. They consist of a dough filled with meat, vegetables, or cheese, then baked or fried. Each region has its own version, but beef empanadas are the most common. They are usually served as a main dish or as a starter.

Ingredients

Below are the ingredients needed for this recipe (12 empanadas):

  • empanada dough discs 12 units
  • ground beef 500g
  • onion 300g
  • green bell pepper 150g
  • oil 30ml
  • hard-boiled eggs 2
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • paprika 5g
  • cumin 3g

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and sauté the onion and bell pepper until soft.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, paprika, and cumin. Mix well and remove from heat.
  4. Let the filling cool, then add the chopped hard-boiled eggs.
  5. Place a spoon of filling in the center of each dough disc.
  6. Fold the dough, seal the edges, and make the repulgue.
  7. Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  8. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until golden brown.
  9. Remove from the oven and serve hot.
